Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling in Laxmi Academy.

Laxmi Academy specialises exclusively in Physics. We offer courses for NEET, JEE Mains, EAMCET, Intermediate 1st Year, and Intermediate 2nd Year. Each course is tailored to the respective exam syllabus and question patterns, with dedicated video lectures, practice tests, and doubt-clearing sessions.

We offer online courses accessible through the Laxmi Academy student portal anytime, anywhere. Video lectures are pre-recorded so you can learn at your own pace and rewatch as many times as you need. For students in Hyderabad, we also conduct in-person doubt-clearing sessions — contact us for the current schedule.

Enrolling is simple: (1) Click "Sign Up" to create your free account. (2) Select your course (NEET, JEE, EAMCET, or Intermediate). (3) Complete payment securely via PhonePe. (4) Your course is instantly activated — log in and start watching! You can also call or WhatsApp us at +91 72078 98999 and we will guide you through the process.

We accept all major UPI apps (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit/credit cards, and net banking — all processed securely through PhonePe's payment gateway. We do not store your card or banking details. If you need to pay via bank transfer or have any payment difficulties, contact us and we will assist you.

We offer a full refund within 7 days of enrollment if you have watched less than 20% of the course. Refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days of approval. For full details, see our Refund Policy.
