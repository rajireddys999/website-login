Data We Collect

When you register or use the NR AI Orbit Learning Portal platform, we collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: full name, email address, phone number, and password (stored in hashed form).
  • Academic details: the exam stream you are enrolled in (NEET, JEE, EAMCET, Intermediate), your class, and any test results.
  • Payment information: transaction IDs and payment status processed via PhonePe. We do not store card or UPI credentials.
  • Usage data: video watch progress, login timestamps, device type, browser, and IP address.
  • Communications: enquiry form submissions and messages sent via WhatsApp or email.
Note: We do not collect sensitive personal information such as Aadhaar numbers, financial account details, or medical records.
2

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to operate and improve the NR AI Orbit Learning Portal learning experience:

  • Provide access to enrolled Physics course videos and learning materials.
  • Track and display your learning progress and test scores.
  • Process payments and send enrollment confirmations.
  • Send important announcements such as new batch dates, schedule changes, and exam reminders.
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support.
  • Analyse aggregate usage to improve course content and platform features.

We will not use your data for unrelated marketing, advertising, or profiling without your explicit consent.

3

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted in India. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Bcrypt hashing for stored passwords — we never store plain-text passwords.
  • JWT-based authentication tokens with short expiry windows.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability checks.
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorised NR AI Orbit Learning Portal staff only.

Data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

4

Student Data

NR AI Orbit Learning Portal takes extra care with student data, particularly for users who may be minors enrolled in Intermediate (11th and 12th grade) courses.

  • Students under 18 should register with parental/guardian knowledge.
  • We do not publish student profiles publicly — all progress and scores are private.
  • Student test performance data is used only for personalised feedback and progress tracking within the portal.
  • We do not share individual student data with third parties, advertisers, or other institutions.
  • Parents or guardians may contact us to request access to or deletion of their child's data.
5

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Payment processors: PhonePe receives transaction details necessary to process payments. Their privacy policy governs how they handle this data.
  • Service providers: Hosting and email delivery providers who are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal obligation: If required by a court order, government authority, or applicable Indian law, we may disclose data to comply with legal obligations.

All third-party partners are required to handle your data in compliance with applicable privacy law.

6

Your Rights

As a user of NR AI Orbit Learning Portal, you have the following rights over your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to specific processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the details below. We will respond within 15 working days.
