Overview

At NR AI Orbit Learning Portal, we believe in the quality of our Physics courses. We offer a fair refund window to give you confidence in your enrollment decision.

Our refund policy is simple: if you are not satisfied within the first 7 days of enrollment and have watched less than 20% of the enrolled course, you are eligible for a full refund of the course fee paid.

The short version: Enrolled within 7 days, watched less than 20% of the course — you get a full refund. No questions asked.
2

Refund Eligibility

Before submitting a refund request, verify that you meet all of the following criteria:

  • Your enrollment date is within 7 calendar days of the refund request date.
  • Your total watch progress for the enrolled course is less than 20% as recorded in the portal.
  • The course fee was paid directly to NR AI Orbit Learning Portal via PhonePe.
  • Your account is in good standing (no violations of our Terms of Service).

Eligible for Refund

  • Within 7 days of payment
  • Less than 20% watched
  • No ToS violations
  • Direct payment via portal

Not Eligible

  • More than 7 days since payment
  • 20% or more of course watched
  • Account suspended or banned
  • Offline / cash payment
3

How to Request a Refund

Refund requests are processed quickly and without hassle. Follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Email us at refunds@laxmiacademy.in with the subject line: "Refund Request – [Your Full Name]"
  • Step 2: Include your registered email address, phone number, course name, and payment transaction ID.
  • Step 3: Briefly describe the reason for your refund request (optional but helpful).
  • Step 4: Our support team will verify eligibility and confirm via email within 2 business days.

You may also contact us via WhatsApp at +91 72078 98999 or by calling during business hours (10 AM – 6 PM, Monday to Saturday).

Do not uninstall or delete your account before the refund is processed — we need your watch progress data to verify eligibility.

5

Exceptions & Special Cases

The following situations are handled on a case-by-case basis by our support team:

  • Technical issues: If you were unable to access course content due to a platform error for an extended period, you may be eligible for a refund or credit regardless of the 7-day window. Please report technical issues immediately.
  • Duplicate payments: If you were charged twice for the same course, the duplicate charge will be refunded in full within 3 business days of verification.
  • Course cancellation: In the unlikely event that a course is cancelled by NR AI Orbit Learning Portal after enrollment, a full refund will be issued automatically within 7 business days.
  • Instalment plans: Refund eligibility for custom instalment arrangements will be governed by the specific agreement made at enrollment.

For exceptional circumstances not covered by this policy, please write to us and we will do our best to find a fair resolution.
